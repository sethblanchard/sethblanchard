In the Abstract

I am an enthusastic Husband, Father, Friend and Hokie. When not involved with those listed, I enjoy rennovating my house, listening to music, making websites, visualizing sports and decreasingly, doing dangerous things out of doors1.

Exploration

A sense of adventure/exploration that I enjoy while out of doors, really drives the work I enjoy doing. I like taking topics I don't know much about and digging in until I understand them.

Translation

While I'm not usually asked to translate from english to spanish (or any other language for that matter), I really enjoy taking topics in one domain and explaining them to those in another. That could be taking design priciples and explaining them to a lay audience, turning sports statistics into easily digestable graphics, or teaching my daughter the best way to make a duplo robot.
